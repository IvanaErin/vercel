North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU) is a public state university located in Tandag City, Surigao del Sur. Formerly Surigao del Sur State University, NEMSU offers a wide variety of academic programs such as Industrial Technology, Agriculture, IT, Business Administration, Education, and more.

Inside the campus lies BiteHub, one of NEMSU’s most accessible and affordable food hubs. BiteHub serves students and staff with freshly prepared meals, snacks, and beverages — ensuring quality and convenience every day.

It operates in two locations inside NEMSU:
• CBM Department Canteen
• CITE Department Canteen
